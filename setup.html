🧩 Jigsaw Puzzle Setup Guide

⚠️ Important: You need to add your puzzle images to get started!

Quick Setup

Step 1: Prepare Your Images Save your two puzzle images (the crypto casino and graveyard scenes) as:
  • images/casino.jpg
  • images/graveyard.jpg
Step 2: Image Requirements
  • Format: JPG or PNG
  • Recommended size: 1920x1080 or higher
  • Aspect ratio: 16:9 works best
Step 3: Launch the Game Open index.html in your web browser
